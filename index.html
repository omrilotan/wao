Wrong Answers Only game

A quiz game where the wrong answers are the right ones.

How to play

  1. You will be given a question, you must answer it with a wrong answer but within the same category.
    For example, if the question is "What is the capital of France?" a good answer could be "Plymouth", a bad answer would be "Grape Soda",
  2. You must answer in a timely manner, if you take too long to answer you will be disqualified.
    Decide on a time limit before you start playing. Consider age and ability of players.
